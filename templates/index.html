How to Play

🎯 Objective

2 Players: Guess all of your opponent's tiles

3-4 Players: Guess the face-down tiles in the center

🎲 Setup

  • Each player gets 4-5 tiles placed behind their screen
  • Tiles are arranged in numerical order (black tiles before white for same numbers)
  • 4 question cards are available in the center for strategic questioning
  • Players take turns asking questions to gather information about other players' tiles

🧩 Tiles

Numbers 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 7, 8, 9 (both white and black) + Two green 5s

Total: 20 tiles (18 regular + 2 special green 5s)

🎯 Gameplay

  • Ask Questions: Use strategic questions to gather information about opponents' tiles
  • Deduce Patterns: Analyze answers to determine tile positions and numbers
  • Make Your Guess: When confident, arrange tiles in the order you think they are
  • Win Conditions: First to correctly guess wins, or everyone gets final chance in some scenarios

🔍 Question Types

  • Counting Questions: "How many white tiles do you have?"
  • Position Questions: "Where are your #5 tiles?"
  • Mathematical: "What is the sum of your tiles?"
  • Conditional: "Is your C tile greater than 4?"
  • Pattern-based: "Which neighboring tiles have consecutive numbers?"
