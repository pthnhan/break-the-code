Guide

Rules and question ideas

Game flow
  • In 2-player games, each player receives 5 hidden tiles and tries to break the opponent's code.
  • In 3-player games, each player receives 5 hidden tiles and the table shares 5 hidden center tiles.
  • In 4-player games, each player receives 4 hidden tiles and the table shares 4 hidden center tiles.
  • On your turn, ask one question from the shared deck or commit to a full guess.
  • When you are confident, submit the complete ordered answer for the correct target.
Question ideas
  • Count colors or numbers.
  • Ask about positions like where the 5 tiles are.
  • Use sums, comparisons, and neighbor patterns to narrow possibilities.
